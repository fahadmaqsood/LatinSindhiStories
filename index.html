Short Sindhi Stories

Written in the Latin Script of Sindhi!

1) Muhunje kapŕan khe khaaiń d̬e

Hik dafo Waẗayo hik door daraz ǵotha men wayal ho. Bukha aen uj̈a laǵi huees, aen ilahi ẗhakal bi ho. Wejho ë unna hik shadi d̬ithi aen shadi hall'a je ander ghiŕi wayo!

Cho ẗa hunna ja kapŕa phaatal hua, hunna khe dhikka d̬ayi shadi'a man kaḍhiyo wayo. Waẗayo cho ẗa hik hoshiyar mańhoon ho, unhen khe jald he sooraẗihal jo andazo laǵi wayo. Unhen pehenje samaan man nawaan kapŕa kaḍhiya aen shaadi'a men wari ghiŕi wayo!

Inna wakẗ Waẗaye jo sabhni sutho isẗakbaal kayo. Jed̬i mehla khaadho shuru ẗhiyo ẗa hoo pehenje kapŕan ẗe haarań laǵo.

Daawaẗ je mezbaanan man hik unhen wat ayo aen chayaeen "O Darwesh'a, chariyo ẗhi payo aheen çha?"

Waẗayo iho b́udhi muskurayo aen chayaeen "Iha saj́i izzaẗa muhunje badraan muhunje kapŕan khe d̬ini huaii na, haańe inhan khe ë khaaiń d̬e".


2) Neth ẗa hed̬ahan bi eenda!

Hik dafo ǵotha men chori ẗhi hui aen sabh mańhoon unhan khe pakŕańa je lae unhan je poyan ḍhoŕi rahya hua.

Waẗayo bi rasẗe je kinare wetho ho aen sabh kujh d̬ithaeen pae. T̈hoŕi der khaan poe uho bi uẗhiyo aen waj̈i kabrusẗan men wahi rahiyo.

Sijj lahińa je wakẗ kujh ǵotha ja mańhoon kabrusẗan wataan langhya aen Waẗaye khe d̬ithaoon, aen puchań laǵa "O Waẗaya! ẗoon hiẗẗe cha payo kareen?"

Waẗaye jawab d̬inoo "sawela ǵotha men chori ẗhi hui, aen aoon hiẗẗe choran jo intazar payo karyan! neth ẗa hed̬ahan bi eenda!".
